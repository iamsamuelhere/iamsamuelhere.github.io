The East Point College of Engineering and Technology EPCET was established under the aegis of M G Charitable Trust founded by the Educational Visionary Dr. S M VenkatPathi, in the year 1999. One of the best colleges for Engineering and Technology in Bangalore, offers courses for under graduate and Post graduate courses. The college holds many records such as producing the university toppers, securing the best job profiles, and crafting the research personalities too. Affiliated to VTU and approved by the AICTE East Point college prides for its highly qualified faculty who comes with their immense knowledge and experience. One of the best campus for placement activities has a record placement in all the reputed national and multinational companies Education in EPCET offers an array of academic programs in a student centered learning environment aids at graduate and post graduate levels with strong emphasis on technology. Education in EPCET is highly committed and responsive to the current trends and needs in the global market and industry. The institute conducts core industry integrated courses and personality development programs. Interactions with industry trends, provides many opportunities for campus placements.
