ABOUT CONFERENCE


This Conference will explore the new scope of innovations from distinguished authors in industry and academia working for the developments in Science, Engineering and Technology from all over the world. The main motivation of conference to bring together Academicians, Scientists, Researcher scholar and students to share and disseminate their knowledge on scientific research related to given topics.The conference confers the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted. By eminent researchers on industry 4.0


CONFERENCE THEME


The fourth Industrial Revolution is emerging and evolving at an exponential growth that will transform entire systems of production management and governance.This conference will highlight the impact of transition to fourth industrial revolution on Infrastructure,ecosystem,efficiency and competitiveness of industrial operation. The impact of robotics, augmented reality,digitization, IOT and artificial intelligent on supply chain support are highlighted.
