Call for papers




ICMSTC 2020, International Conference on Industry 4.0, provides a forum for researchers, academicians, professionals, and students from various engineering fields and with cross-disciplinary interests and working in Industry 4.0,to interact and disseminate information on the latest development
Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers for presentation at the conference and publication in the conference proceedings. Accepted papers will be published in the ICMSTC ’2020 Conference Proceedings and presented papers will be submitted to SPRINGER.
ICMSTC ’2020 invites papers, original & unpublished work from individuals active in the broad theme of the conference. Potential topics include, but are not limited to:
